The Story Behind My Sente

Rodgers Magabo - Indie developer

I am Rodgers Magabo, an indie developer with a passion for understanding and improving personal finances. My journey into the world of app development started with a personal challenge: I wanted to learn how I was spending my money, find effective ways to pay off my debts, and grow my net worth.

This quest led me to teach myself coding and design, skills that were crucial in bringing my vision to life.The result of this journey is 'My Sente', an app born from my own need and now a tool I am proud to share with the world. My goal with 'My Sente' is straightforward – to help others gain clarity on their financial habits, particularly highlighting how taxes can be their biggest expense.

I believe that understanding and managing one's finances is a key step towards financial freedom.'My Sente' is more than just an app; it's a reflection of my belief in the power of self-education and the impact technology can have in transforming our lives.

By sharing this app, I hope to assist many in navigating their financial journeys more effectively, empowering them to make informed decisions and achieve their financial goals. My journey from grappling with personal finance issues to developing a solution that can help others is a testament to what one can achieve with determination and a desire to make a difference.
